The Best Movie According to JC

My Top 3 Movies

Wicked

Elphaba, whose skin is green, and Glinda, who is blonde and beautiful.
They dislike each other at first but soon form an unlikely bond.
The musical follows them from their girlhood friendship and explains
how Elphaba became the Wicked Witch and Glinda became the Good Witch.

Business Proposal

a woman who goes on a blind date in disguise to scare away a potential suitor,
but ends up marrying her CEO. The show is based on a webtoon and web novel of the same name.

Hotarubi no mori e

This is a Japanese animated film and manga about a young girl who gets
lost in a forest inhabited by spirits. The story is a romance that
explores themes of love, loss, and the human-spirit connection.
